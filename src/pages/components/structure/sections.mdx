## Semantic Sections
```html
<div class="section-container allpad">
  <section>
    <div class="section-title">The Section</div>
    <div class="section-content">Duis aute irure dolor in reprehenderit in voluptate velit esse
		  cillum dolore eu fugiat nulla pariatur. Excepteur sint occaecat cupidatat non
		  proident, sunt in culpa qui officia deserunt mollit anim id est laborum.</div>
  </section>
  <section>
    <div class="section-title">The Other Section</div>
    <section class="subsection">
      <div class="subsection-title">The Subsection</div>
      <div class="subsection-content">Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipisicing elit, sed do eiusmod
        tempor incididunt ut labore</div>
    </section>
    <section class="subsection">
      <div class="subsection-title">The Second Subsection</div>
      <div class="subsection-content">Dolore magna aliqua. Ut enim ad minim veniam,
			  quis nostrud exercitation</div>
    </section>
  </section>
</div>
```

## Standard Sections
```html
<div class="section-container allpad">
	<div class="section">
		<h2 class="section-title">The Section</h2>
		<div class="section-content">Duis aute irure dolor in reprehenderit in voluptate velit esse
			cillum dolore eu fugiat nulla pariatur. Excepteur sint occaecat cupidatat non
			proident, sunt in culpa qui officia deserunt mollit anim id est laborum.</div> 	
	</div>
	<div class="section">
		<h2 class="section-title">The Other Section</h2>
		<div class="subsection">
			<h3 class="subsection-title">The Subsection</h3>
			<div class="subsection-content">Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipisicing elit, sed do eiusmod
				tempor incididunt ut labore.</div>
		</div>
		<div class="subsection">
			<h3 class="subsection-title">The Second Subsection</h3>
			<div class="subsection-content">Dolore magna aliqua. Ut enim ad minim veniam,
				quis nostrud exercitation ullamco laboris nisi ut aliquip ex ea commodo
				consequat.</div>
		</div>
	</div>
</div>
```
