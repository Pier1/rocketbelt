# Typography

## Tips

- Use semantics to provide structure to the document.
- Never break rank just for font sizing or weight. The next heading after an h1 should
  be an h2 even if that h2 is much smaller. Use HTML for meaning and CSS for
  styling. You can make that h2 whatever size you need.

## Typefaces
### Sans Serif
[Nunito Sans]{.sans .font-bold} is Rocketbelt's sans serif typeface.

```scss code-only
font-family: font-family(sans);
```

### Serif
[EB Garamond]{.serif .font-bold .font-plus1} is Rocketbelt's serif typeface.

```scss code-only
font-family: font-family(serif);
```

## Lists
[List Reset]{.subhead-unlinked}
```html
<ul class="list-reset">
  <li>One</li>
  <li>Two
    <ul>
      <li>A</li>
      <li>B</li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li>Three</li>
</ul>
```

## Modifiers
### Ellipses

[Single Line]{.subhead-unlinked}
```html
<div class="ellipsis">
  The fabric that’s as it were salvaged from polyester fabric, our framed piece
  enhances the hidden zipper.
</div>
```

[Multiline]{.subhead-unlinked}
* Manipulate the `-webkit-line-clamp` property to change the number of visible
  lines.
```html
<div class="ellipsis-multiline">
  Look again at that dot. That’s here. That's home. That’s us. On it everyone
  you love, everyone you know, everyone you ever heard of, every human being who
  ever was, lived out their lives. The aggregate of our joy and suffering,
  thousands of confident religions, ideologies, and economic doctrines, every
  hunter and forager, every hero and coward, every creator and destroyer of
  civilization, every king and peasant, every young couple in love, every mother
  and father, hopeful child, inventor and explorer, every teacher of morals,
  every corrupt politician, every “superstar,” every “supreme leader,” every
  saint and sinner in the history of our species lived there—on a mote of dust
  suspended in a sunbeam.
</div>
```

### Text with Icon
Note the use of the `aria-hidden` attribute on the `svg` element. As long as the
text is descriptive—and the icon itself is decorative—`aria-hidden` should be
present.

Use the `icon-left` and `icon-right` classes to indicate icon placement.
```html
<span class="text-with-icon">Store Locator
  <svg class="icon icon-left" aria-hidden>
    <use xlink:href="/icons/rocketbelt.icons.svg#rb-icon-pin"></use>
  </svg>
</span>
<br />
<a class="text-with-icon" href="#">Chat Now
  <svg class="icon icon-right" aria-hidden>
    <use xlink:href="/icons/rocketbelt.icons.svg#rb-icon-chat-single"></use>
  </svg>
</a>
```
