Cards

Basic Cards

Cards are building blocks that lead to more detailed information.Their use, size, and composition can vary greatly.
Cards should be organized using grid classes, and can contain many types of content.

                                <div class="row xs-center font-weight-light">
                                  <div class="col xs-3">
                                    <div class="card">
                                      Cards are building blocks that lead to more detailed information.Their use, size, and composition can vary greatly.</div>
                                  </div>
                                  <div class="col xs-3">
                                    <div class="card">Cards should be organized using <span class="font-monospace">grid</span> classes, and can contain many types of content.</div>
                                  </div>
                                </div>
