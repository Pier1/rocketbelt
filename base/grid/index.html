Grid

Here is some short front matter about the grid.

Simple Syntax

Get started with as few as four classes. 

                              <div class="grid-fluid">
                                <div class="row">
                                  <div class="col xs"></div>
                                </div>
                              </div>

Mobile-First & Responsive

Start with xs, then mix and match responsive modifiers. 

                              <div class="grid-fluid">
                                <div class="row">
                                  <div class="col xs-12 md-2 xl-4"></div>
                                  <div class="col xs-6 md-2 xl-4"></div>
                                  <div class="col xs-6 md-2 xl-4"></div>
                                  <div class="col xs-12 md-2 xl-3"></div>
                                  <div class="col xs-3 md-2 xl-3"></div>
                                  <div class="col xs-3 md-2 xl-3"></div>
                                  <div class="col xs-3 md-4 xl-3"></div>
                                  <div class="col xs-3 md-4 xl-6"></div>
                                  <div class="col xs-12 md-4 xl-6"></div>
                                  <div class="col xs-6 md-6 xl-12"></div>
                                  <div class="col xs-6 md-6 xl-12"></div>
                                  <div class="col xs-12 md-12 xl-12"></div>
                                </div>
                              </div>

Automatic Widths

Leave off a column width if it needs to grow or shrink. The grid will size it automatically. 

                              <div class="grid-fluid">
                                <div class="row">
                                  <div class="col xs-1"></div>
                                  <div class="col xs"></div>
                                </div>
                                <div class="row">
                                  <div class="col xs-3"></div>
                                  <div class="col xs"></div>
                                  <div class="col xs-3"></div>
                                </div>
                                <div class="row">
                                  <div class="col xs xl-1"></div>
                                  <div class="col xs xl"></div>
                                  <div class="col xs xl-1"></div>
                                </div>
                              </div>

Column Offsets

Nudge a column to the right with an `-offset` class. 

                              <div class="grid-fluid">
                                <div class="row">
                                  <div class="col xs-offset-2 xs-4 md-offset-3 md-6 xl-offset-8 xl-4"></div>
                                </div>
                                <div class="row">
                                  <div class="col xs-offset-3 xs-4 md-offset-3 md-6 xl-offset-6 xl-4"></div>
                                </div>
                                <div class="row">
                                  <div class="col xs-offset-4 xs-4 md-offset-3 md-6 xl-offset-4 xl-4"></div>
                                </div>
                                <div class="row">
                                  <div class="col xs-offset-5 xs-4 md-offset-3 md-6 xl-offset-2 xl-4"></div>
                                </div>
                              </div>

Nesting


                              <div class="grid-fluid">
                                <div class="row">
                                  <div class="col xs-8">
                                    <div class="row">
                                      <div class="col xs"></div>
                                    </div>
                                  </div>
                                  <div class="col xs-4"></div>
                                </div>
                              </div>

Alignment Classes

Horizontal

-start, -center, -end


                              <div class="grid-fluid">
                                <div class="row xs-start md-center xl-end">
                                  <div class="col xs-4"></div>
                                </div>
                              </div>

Vertical

-top, -middle, -bottom


                              <div class="grid-fluid">
                                <div class="row xs-top md-middle xl-bottom">
                                  <div class="col xs-6 example-col-tall"></div>
                                  <div class="col xs-6"></div>
                                </div>
                              </div>

Column Distribution

space-between

Space columns evenly across the row, with no space at the beginning or end of the row. 

                              <div class="grid-fluid">
                                <div class="row xs-between">
                                  <div class="col xs-2"></div>
                                  <div class="col xs-2"></div>
                                  <div class="col xs-2"></div>
                                </div>
                              </div>

space-around

Space columns evenly across the row, including space at the beginning & end of the row. 

                              <div class="grid-fluid">
                                <div class="row xs-around">
                                  <div class="col xs-2"></div>
                                  <div class="col xs-2"></div>
                                  <div class="col xs-2"></div>
                                </div>
                              </div>

Column Order

first & last


                              <div class="grid-fluid">
                                <div class="row">
                                  <div class="col xs-2"></div>
                                  <div class="col xs-2"></div>
                                  <div class="col xs-2"></div>
                                  <div class="col xs-2"></div>
                                  <div class="col xs-2"></div>
                                  <div class="col xs-2 xs-first"></div>
                                </div>
                              </div>

                              <div class="grid-fluid">
                                <div class="row">
                                  <div class="col xs-2 xs-last"></div>
                                  <div class="col xs-2"></div>
                                  <div class="col xs-2"></div>
                                  <div class="col xs-2"></div>
                                  <div class="col xs-2"></div>
                                  <div class="col xs-2"></div>
                                </div>
                              </div>

                                  <div class="col xs-2">1</div>

                                  <div class="col xs-2">4</div>
                                  <div class="col xs-2">5</div>
                                  <div class="col xs-2">6</div>
                                </div>
